Conservation of Electric Charge

Electric charge cannot be created or destroyed - it can only be transferred from one object to another. This is called the Law of Conservation of Charge.

Key Principle:

The total amount of positive and negative charge in the universe always stays the same. When we "create" charge, we're actually separating existing positive and negative charges.
